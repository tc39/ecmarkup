Internal Function

Refs

Can refer to functions in prose, eg. Internal.

Internal works if Internal is in the middle of the prose.

Don't autolink references inside square brackets, eg: [[Internal]]. Likewise, percent things: %%Internal%%. Likewise, mentions of Internal.foo.
